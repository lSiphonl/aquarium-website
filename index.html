Finfinity Aquarium

Finfinity Aquarium is a world-class marine theme park located in Durban, South Africa. It combines a large aquarium, exciting water attractions, restaurants, and interactive marine experiences, offering visitors the opportunity to explore ocean life while learning about the importance of marine conservation in an entertaining environment. Guests can discover fascinating species from around the world, enjoy educational exhibits, and take part in unforgettable animal encounters suitable for all ages. For the true ocean life fan, there are designated merchandise shops located throughout the park, offering a wide range of souvenirs, apparel, and marine-themed collectibles to remember your visit.
