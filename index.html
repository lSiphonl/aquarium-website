Finfinity Aquarium

Finfinity Aquarium is a world-class marine theme park located in Durban, South Africa. It combines a large aquarium, exciting water attractions, restaurants, and marine experiences, offering visitors the opportunity to explore ocean life and learn about marine conservation in an entertaining environment. For the true ocean life fan, there are designated merchandise shops across the whole of the park.
