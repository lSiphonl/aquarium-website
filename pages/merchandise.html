Did You Know?

Sharks have existed for more than 400 million years, meaning they were swimming in Earth's oceans long before dinosaurs appeared. Despite their fearsome reputation, many shark species are gentle creatures that play an important role in keeping marine ecosystems balanced by controlling populations of other animals.

Did You Know?

Octopuses are considered some of the most intelligent creatures in the ocean. They have three hearts, blue blood, and the ability to solve puzzles, open containers, and even remember solutions to problems they have faced before. Their incredible ability to camouflage helps them hide from predators and sneak up on prey.

Did You Know?

Sea turtles have been swimming in Earth's oceans for over 100 million years. Some species can travel thousands of kilometres during migration, returning to the same beaches where they were born to lay their own eggs.
